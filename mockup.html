NPSymbols

NPSymbols are the custom web map icon set for the National Park Service. We integrate NPSymbols into our other mapping tools, including Places Editor, Park Tiles, and Builder. NPSymbols' clear and distinctive graphic identity originates with the map symbols developed for NPS print maps by the Harper's Ferry Center. Special modifications have been made to each icon to optimize its visibility on web maps. Icons optimized to display NPS points of interest as an overlay in Park Tiles are designed to be stand-alone icons, displayed without a bounding box or placemarker symbol. In addition, we use the Maki toolset developed by Mapbox to adapt NPSymbols for display within tear-drop style markers inside Builder and Places Editor. Supporting both black and white versions assures we can render NPSymbols clearly over a wide range of background colors.
